About EVADE

The access to EnVironmental Analytics for Developing countriEs (EVADE) project builds on STFC and NERC investments into the Community Intercomparison Suite to develop a demonstration of an easy-to-use web portal providing access to environmental analytics of "big" Earth System data to users and scientists in developing countries (maximising co-benefits for the UK scientific and commercial applications).

This site is intended to demonstrate these concepts and explore the technical challenges in realising this ambition.

Funding

Funding for this initial demonstration phase was provided by STFC (ST/P003206/1). CIS is developed as open-source software and deployed for community use on the JASMIN platform provided by UK NERC, the UK Space Agency and the Science and Technology Facilities Council.
